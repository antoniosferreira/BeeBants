BeeBants

Self-learning project with the purpose of discoverig Swift and iOS programming environments. It was also an opportunity to introduce myself to Google's Firebase services.

About BeeBants

Aren't you fed up of spending hours deciding where to go? So are we! Which is why we came up with Bee Bants. An app that makes the decision for you! Build your own profile and have us choose the perfect location for you! Get in touch with your adventurous side!
